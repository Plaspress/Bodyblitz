About Us:

"Our Goal Is Your Goal"

Body Blitz Fitness owner and operator, John Dessent says,

" We dont cut corners when it comes to providing this type of service. We buy the best equipment, we hire the best staff, we have a great friendly facility, and we provide something for everybody from the newcomer to the veteran. We want everybody to feel welcome and satisfied that their health investment reaches its full potential ".

At Body Blitz Fitness, we understand that it can be intimidating walking into a gym for the first time. For this reason, we will provide you with a fitness and healthy eating program to help even the most novice exerciser to get started. With a background in professional body building and experience exercising in gyms, John knows how to provide the best training equipment, environment and facility to make your exercise experience a positive one. We recognise that everybody has individual needs, and everybody makes different choices to suit their ever-changing lives. Some people are self motivated to achieve certain goals. Others need support and inspiration to guide them through. We provide programs, facilities and services to ensure our members get the most out of their membership.
Getting Started Our friendly staff will provide you with all the information needed to get you off to a solid start. Our qualified instructors can take you through a structured workout, covering the basic principles of exercise. We will also guide you through our Group Fitness programmes and we will help you choose the most appropriate Group Fitness class to achieve your health and fitness goals.
We offer this service FREE for all new members.
With over two decades in the fitness industry, John and the team are well placed to help you achieve your goals.
